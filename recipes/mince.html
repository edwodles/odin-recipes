Mince

Description

My taco mince is an easy way to get a protein source in you and only take 12 minutes to prepare!

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Olive Oil
  • 100ml Water

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in pan on medium/high heat
  2. Fry mince
  3. Once brown, add taco seasoning
  4. Add water and let simmer on medium/high until liquid has reduced
