Chicken

Description

My 17 minute a side chicken is a sure fire way to get a great protein source in without lifting a finger!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Masterfoods Moroccan Seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celcius
  2. Line baking tray with baking paper
  3. Season chicken generously with Masterfoods Moroccan Seasoning
  4. Cook 17 minutes each side
  5. Remove from oven and rest, covered, for 10 minutes
