Overnight Oats

Description

Simple, quick and cheap, my overnight oats will have you thinking about me all night until you can open up the fridge and eat me up!

Ingredients

  • 50g Rolled Oats
  • 1 banana, chopped
  • 1 scoop of protein powder of choice
  • 50g frozen berries
  • 120ml milk of choice

Steps

  1. Combine oats, milk, and protein powder in a bowl and mix until combined
  2. Add banana and berries
  3. Cover and soak in the fridge for at least 2 hours
